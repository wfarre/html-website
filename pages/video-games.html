Video Games

Here is my top 3

  1. kingdom Hearts

    I am a great fan of Final Fantasy, so when I learned about they will mix the universe of Final Fantasy and the one of Disney, I thought: "this will never work, it's stupid". BUT, I am curious, I decided to give it a chance and within 5 days, I finished it, I loved it. The gameplay was classic and original in the same time. The scenario was interesting enough, and less complicated that the rest of the saga. And what a pleasure to revisit the Disney movies as one of the main characters.

  2. Baldur's Gate III

    I am more Japanese Role Playing Gamer, but Baldur's Gate III is such an incredible experience. I have always dreamt about playing Dungeons and Dragons, so this a great opportunity. I played the first one a little bit a long time ago, but I think the third one is a total success: it is beautiful, the story-telling is incredible. And about the story-telling, which is the strength of the game, is so crazy: you have so much freedom in the game, so you really feel that you are playing and not watching an interactive movie. This is one of my problem with the recent games, there is still some challenge, but you don't feel as player, you are THAT important, and I feel BG3 put the player back to the center of the game.

  3. The Last of Us Part II

    I can't talk video games without talking a Naughty Dog's game. The Last of Us, part II, was disturbing and put myself in a little bit uncomfortable position. The story was great and very interesting, making you questionning about what/who is good, what/who is bad.
